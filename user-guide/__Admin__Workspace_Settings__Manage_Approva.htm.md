###  Manage Approval Status

In workspaces with the Approval Submittal module enabled, Workspace
Administrators can determine which approval statuses are available for use.

Note  You cannot add or remove approval statuses from this master list.

  1. In the Workspace Settings pane, click on Manage Approval Status. 
  2. The available approval statuses are listed, indicating whether they are currently active or inactive. 
  3. To change the active status, select an approval status and click on Active/Inactive.
